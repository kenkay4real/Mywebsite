About the Author

Kenny Kay (born March 4, 2002) is an Algerian poet and author whose passion for storytelling emerged early in life. Growing up in a bustling city, he found refuge in books and indulged in the fantastical worlds that sprang from his imagination.

Kay’s writing weaves raw, emotional narratives in both poetry and lyrical prose—exploring themes of grief, love, longing, and introspection with minimalist yet profound language.

Latest Release

Faces is a journey through the raw and vulnerable emotions that shape our identity. Kenny Kay’s poetry exposes the many faces we wear, the masks we hide behind, and the truths we long to reveal. Each poem resonates with authenticity and captures moments of love, loss, and self-discovery. A compelling collection for those seeking to connect with the unspoken depths of the soul.
